## **Mirror opposite triangle**
Write a program to print triangle with mirror for N numbers. 


#### **Sample Input**
    N = 5

#### **Sample Output**
             *
           * * *
         * * * * *
       * * * * * * *
     * * * * * * * * *
       * * * * * * *
         * * * * * 
           * * * 
             *

#### **Expected Time Complexity**
__O__(N*2), constant time complexity 
#### **Expected Space Complexity**
__O__(1), constant space complexity
